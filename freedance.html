Free Dance for Creativity & Healing

Dance Movement Therapy is used to help individuals struggling with stress and anxiety through well-structured improvisational movement with music, typically led by dance movement therapist. It is extremely beneficial for those suffering from anxiety, depression, PTSD, and more.

Click here to learn more about the benefits of Dance Therapy!
