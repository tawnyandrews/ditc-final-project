Build Your Own Brain Dance

The "Brain Dance" is a full body-brain exercise consisting of a sequence of eight fundamental developmental movement patterns that help to "reorganize our central nervous system", improving cognition, increasing attention and focus, reducing fatigue and stress, and simply gets our bodies moving.

What is the Brain Dance Made Up of?

The Brain Dance consists of eight fundamental steps:

  1. Breath
  2. Tactile Touch
  3. Core Distal Body Extention
  4. Head-Tail Movement
  5. Upper-Lower Movement
  6. Body-Side Movement
  7. Cross-Lateral
  8. Vestibular
Feel free to watch the following video to get an idea of how to build your own Brain Dance and then try it out yourself! Your Brain Dance can be whatever feels good for you and you can change the order of the steps as you please!
